# Orbit self-initiated projects — Instagram captions

## 01 — Signal Atlas

Signal Atlas — a self-initiated identity study for explainable AI.

Instead of presenting intelligence as magic, we treated every source and decision as a traceable coordinate. Neural cartography, orbital motion and one black core form a visual system for technology that should be understood—not merely trusted.

Identity / Data visualization / Motion direction

## 02 — Morrow Objects

Morrow Objects — objects designed for another life.

A self-initiated circular homeware brand built around modular vessels, visible material stories and a campaign that refuses the familiar “quiet sustainability” aesthetic. Warm, strange and made to be rearranged.

Brand strategy / 3D art direction / Campaign

#BrandIdentity #CircularDesign #3DDesign #ArtDirection #ProductDesign #DesignInspiration

## 03 — Tidal Commons

Tidal Commons — coastal risk made shared.

This self-initiated climate-data platform turns layered topography, water movement and projected scenarios into one public visual language. Cobalt fields carry the landscape; acid contours make change visible.

Data design / Product UX / Mapping

#ClimateDesign #DataDesign #InformationDesign #UXDesign #DigitalProduct #CreativeCoding

## 04 — Relay Field

Relay Field — every field action survives offline.

An offline-first logistics concept for places where weak connectivity is normal, not exceptional. Every task, scan and delivery proof enters a visible queue, connected by one uninterrupted signal.

Service design / Mobile UX / Systems identity

#ProductDesign #LogisticsTech #MobileDesign #UXUI #ServiceDesign #TechBranding

## 06 — Form Zero

Form Zero — material behaviour becomes interface.

Compression, liquid flow and architectural folding meet inside one continuous sculpture. A self-initiated campaign and WebGL concept for adaptive materials that are defined by what they do, not how they look when still.
